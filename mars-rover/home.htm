The Mars Rover Program, An Introduction.

NASA's Mars Exploration Program (MEP) is a program spanning decades, aimed at exploring the beauty of Mars and discovering more about our universe that we would not be aware of without our Rovers.

These robot helpers are meant to explore the surface of Mars and bring back any data that would help us understand the planet better. While they may not return today, nor tomorrow, their results will speak for a lifetime when they return.

Meet our little robot helpers

Curiosity

Perogative: Determine if Mars could support microbial life.

Perseverance

Perogative: Discover signs of ancient life and collect samples for the return to Earth.
