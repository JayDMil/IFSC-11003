Game Overview

Dragon Raid is an indie action project focused on turn-based combat, asciiart, and a sense of roguelike adventure. This site shares a quick overview, the development roadmap, and answers to common questions.

Development is iterative and public—feedback and issue reports help shape balance, readability, and encounter design.

Key Features

  • Core Gameplay

    Turn-based combat with a focus on randomized builds and items that stay with you for your playthrough.

  • Progression

    Skills and Equipment that will give you different ways to battle in combat as you clear more zones.

  • World & Story

    A high-fantasy world with a grim undertone, enticing the player to always try to explore more.
